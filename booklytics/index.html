INTRODUCTION

Booklytics is an interactive e-commerce analytics dashboard designed for both book lovers and bookstore managers. By integrating data from the Google Books API and capturing real-time user engagement, our platform offers a unique, data-driven book browsing experience. Whether you're searching for your next favorite read or analyzing reading trends, Booklytics brings insights and discovery together in one powerful interface.

Read about our team

PURPOSE

The purpose of Booklytics is to bridge the gap between book discovery and data intelligence. We aim to create a platform that empowers readers to interact with books through reviews, ratings, and likes, while also providing book owners with meaningful analytics to understand market trends, popular genres, and user preferences.
